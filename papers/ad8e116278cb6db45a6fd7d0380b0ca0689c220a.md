# Supporting Mouthing in Signed Languages: New innovations and a proposal for future corpus building

**Year:** [[2022]]
**Topics:** [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]], [[topics/linguistics|Linguistics]]
**Authors:** Rosalee J. Wolfe; John C. McDonald; Ronan Johnson; Ben Sturr; Sydney Klinghoffer; Anthony Bonzani; Andrew Alexander; Nicole Barnekow

## Links

## TL;DR

Efforts to improve avatar mouthing by addressing challenges, resulting in a new approach for mOUthing animation are described, and an experiment in corpus building using the new approach is proposed.
