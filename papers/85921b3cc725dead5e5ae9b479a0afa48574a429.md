## TL;DR

Author Padden and Humphries have undertaken the task of clarifying the cultural "uniqueness" of the deaf, but their approach is less rigorous than traditional approaches by cultural anthropologists or linguists, and this in some ways detracts from the structured scholarship that such credentials afford.
