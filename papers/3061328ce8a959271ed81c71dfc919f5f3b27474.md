# The Synthesis of Complex Shape Deployments in Sign Language

**Year:** [[2020]]
**Topics:** [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]], [[topics/linguistics|Linguistics]]
**Authors:** Michael Filhol; John C. McDonald

## Links
- [Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org/paper/3061328ce8a959271ed81c71dfc919f5f3b27474)
- [ACL Anthology](https://aclanthology.org/2020.signlang-1.10)

## TL;DR

This paper continues to show that with a small number of services in a signing avatar, these descriptions can be synthesized in a natural way that captures the essential gestural actions while also including the subtleties of human motion that make the signing legible.
