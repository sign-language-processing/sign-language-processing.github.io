# Deep Sign: Hybrid CNN-HMM for Continuous Sign Language Recognition

**Year:** [[2016]]
**Topics:** [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]], [[topics/linguistics|Linguistics]]
**Authors:** Oscar Koller; Sepehr Zargaran; H. Ney; R. Bowden

## Links
- [Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org/paper/31b4f2a9fcd8e45a5b4f4cf95e3caa8b460c062f)
- [DOI](https://doi.org/10.5244/C.30.136)

## TL;DR

This paper introduces the end-to-end embedding of a CNN into a HMM, while interpreting the outputs of the CNN in a Bayesian fashion, to improve over the state-of-the-art on three challenging benchmark continuous sign language recognition tasks.
