# Avatar animation from SignWriting notation

**Year:** [[2015]]
**Topics:** [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]], [[topics/linguistics|Linguistics]]
**Authors:** K. Abrahams

## Links
- [Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org/paper/0200b8ded1800fa41bb5b6dd76c33750f00c0c2e)

## TL;DR

The specific focus of the research is achieving animations that are accurate representations of the SignWriting input in terms of the five fundamental parameters of sign language, namely, hand motion, location, orientation and shape, as well as non-manual features such as facial expressions.
