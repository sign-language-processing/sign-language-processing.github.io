# Beyond Words: AuralLLM and SignMST-C for Precise Sign Language Production and Bidirectional Accessibility

**Year:** [[2025]]
**Topics:** [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]], [[topics/linguistics|Linguistics]]

## TL;DR

CNText2Sign and CNSign are introduced, comprehensive datasets to benchmark SLP and SLT, respectively, with CNText2Sign covering gloss and landmark mappings for SLP, and CNSign providing extensive video-to-text data for SLT.
