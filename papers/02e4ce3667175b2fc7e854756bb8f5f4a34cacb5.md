**Year:** [[2015]]
**Topics:** [[topics/medicine|Medicine]], [[topics/education|Education]], [[topics/linguistics|Linguistics]]

## TL;DR

It was found that 48% of Deaf participants had inadequate health literacy, and Deaf individuals were 6.9 times more likely than hearing participants to have inadequate health Literacy.
