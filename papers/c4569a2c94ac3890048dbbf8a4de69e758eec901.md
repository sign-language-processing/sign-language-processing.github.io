# Automatic sign language recognition: from video corpora to gloss sentences

**Year:** [[2020]]
**Topics:** [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]], [[topics/linguistics|Linguistics]]
**Authors:** Jens Forster

## Links
- [Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org/paper/c4569a2c94ac3890048dbbf8a4de69e758eec901)

## TL;DR

This PhD thesis investigates large vocabulary, continuous automatic sign language recognition (ASLR) from single view video using hidden Markov models (HMMs) with Gaussian mixture models (GMMs) as state emission functions and n-gram, statistical language models to address multimodal nature of sign languages.
