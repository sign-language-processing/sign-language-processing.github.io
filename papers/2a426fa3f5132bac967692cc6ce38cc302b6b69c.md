# Deep JSLC: A Multimodal Corpus Collection for Data-driven Generation of Japanese Sign Language Expressions

## TL;DR

A corpus of continuous sentence utterances in Japanese Sign Language (JSL) applicable to the learning of deep neural network models and shows the ability to train relevant language features.
