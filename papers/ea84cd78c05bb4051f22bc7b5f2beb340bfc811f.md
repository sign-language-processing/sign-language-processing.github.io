# BosphorusSign: A Turkish Sign Language Recognition Corpus in Health and Finance Domains

**Year:** [[2016]]
**Topics:** [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]], [[topics/medicine|Medicine]]
**Authors:** Necati Cihan Camgöz; A. Kındıroglu; Serpil Karabüklü; Meltem Kelepir; A. S. Özsoy; L. Akarun

## Links

## TL;DR

The BosphorusSign Turkish Sign Language corpus is presented, which consists of 855 sign and phrase samples from the health, finance and everyday life domains and will be the first in this sign language research field using the state-of-the-art Microsoft Kinect v2 depth sensor.
