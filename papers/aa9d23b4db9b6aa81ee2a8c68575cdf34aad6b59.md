# Isolated Video-Based Arabic Sign Language Recognition Using Convolutional and Recursive Neural Networks

**Year:** [[2021]]
**Topics:** [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]], [[topics/linguistics|Linguistics]]
**Authors:** Abdelbasset Boukdir; M. Benaddy; Ayoub Ellahyani; O. E. Meslouhi; Mustapha Kardouchi

## TL;DR

A novel approach based on a deep learning architecture to classify video sequences of Arabic signlanguage, especially Moroccan sign language, with level accuracy of 92% for 2DCRNN and 99% for 3DCNN.
