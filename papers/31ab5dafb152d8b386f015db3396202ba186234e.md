# British Sign Language Corpus Project: A corpus of digital video data and annotations of British Sign Language 2008-2014

**Year:** [[2014]]
**Topics:** [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]], [[topics/linguistics|Linguistics]]
**Authors:** A. Schembri; J. Fenlon; R. Rentelis; Kearsy Cormier

## Links
- [Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org/paper/31ab5dafb152d8b386f015db3396202ba186234e)

## TL;DR

The British Sign Language Corpus Project dataset is an online collection of video clips showing Deaf people using BSL, together with background information about the signers, and some annotations, which is one of the largest sign language corpora in the world, and the second to be made available online (after the Netherlands).
