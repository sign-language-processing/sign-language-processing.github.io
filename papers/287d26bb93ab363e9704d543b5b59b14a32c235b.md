# Detecting Reduplication in Videos of American Sign Language

**Year:** [[2012]]
**Topics:** [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]], [[topics/linguistics|Linguistics]]
**Authors:** Zoya Gavrilov; S. Sclaroff; C. Neidle; S. Dickinson

## TL;DR

The apriori algorithm for mining frequent patterns in data streams is adapted for finding reduplication in videos of ASL and the apriori algorithm is extended to allow for inexact matching of similar hand motion subsequences and to provide robustness to noise.
