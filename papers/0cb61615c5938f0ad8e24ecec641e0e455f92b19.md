**Year:** [[2015]]
**Topics:** [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]], [[topics/linguistics|Linguistics]]

## TL;DR

In this paper, lemmatisation practices used in the creation of BSL SignBank, a lexical database and dictionary of British Sign Language based on signs identified within the British Sign language Corpus are outlined.
