# Manifestations of SLI in LSF: Focus on argument structures

**Year:** [[2015]]
**Topics:** [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]], [[topics/linguistics|Linguistics]]
**Authors:** L. Puissant-Schontz; C. Fortuna; Marion Blondel

## Links
- [Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org/paper/028571d92f3166e28c5428ae6bc0d99f1fb421d8)

## TL;DR

A new typology of predicative structures is developed and presented, focusing on indices, to access the distinctive meaning of these structures, possibly used in simultaneous or sequential combination: floating Vs anchored, orientation, itinerary, configuration.
