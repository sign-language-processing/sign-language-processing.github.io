## Abstract

Overcoming communication barriers and ensuring effective information exchange are crucial for individuals with hearing loss, as they play a key role in fostering social integration. Recent advances in computer vision have enabled the development of various applications for sign language processing, designed to address the challenges posed by this sensory disability in daily life. These tools facilitate accurate communication by interpreting sign language across different formats. In this context, we present a comprehensive review of state-of-the-art computer vision approaches powered by AI technologies for sign language processing, highlighting their strengths and limitations while offering insights into current progress and future directions.
