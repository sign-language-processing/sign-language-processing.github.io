# Approaches to the Anonymisation of Sign Language Corpora

**Year:** [[2020]]
**Topics:** [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]], [[topics/linguistics|Linguistics]]
**Authors:** Amy Isard

## Links
- [Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org/paper/e452ac1031ba5aae574c4dbaa9cafaab91c55dce)

## TL;DR

The motivations behind anonymisation and the close connection with the issue of ethics and informed consent for corpus participants are explored and the variety of ways in which these can be carried out are described.
