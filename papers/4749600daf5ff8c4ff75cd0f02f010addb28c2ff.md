# Efficient voice activity detection algorithms using long-term speech information

**Year:** [[2004]]
**Topics:** [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]], [[topics/engineering|Engineering]]
**Authors:** J. Ramírez; J. C. Segura; C. Benítez; A. de la Torre; A. Rubio

## TL;DR

A new VAD algorithm for improving speech detection robustness in noisy environments and the performance of speech recognition systems is presented, which formsulates the speech/non-speech decision rule by comparing the long-term spectral envelope to the average noise spectrum, thus yielding a high discriminating decision rule and minimizing the average number of decision errors.
