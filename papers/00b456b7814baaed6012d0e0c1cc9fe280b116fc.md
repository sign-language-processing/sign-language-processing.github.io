# iSign: An Architecture for Humanoid Assisted Sign Language Tutoring

**Year:** [[2015]]
**Topics:** [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]], [[topics/education|Education]]
**Authors:** H. Kose-Bagci; Neziha Akalin; Rabia Yorganci; B. Ertugrul; Hasan Kivrak; Semih Kavak; A. Özkul; Cemal Gurpinar; Pinar Uluer; G. Ince

## TL;DR

A computational framework is designed to motivate the children with communication problems to understand and imitate the signs implemented by the robot using the basic upper torso gestures and sound in a turn-taking manner to endow the robot the capability of perceiving the actions of the children.
