# MyoSL: A Framework for Measuring Usability of Two-Arm Gestural Electromyography for Sign Language

**Year:** [[2018]]
**Topics:** [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]]
**Authors:** Jordan Aiko Deja; Patrick Arceo; D. David; Patrick Lawrence Gan; R. C. Roque

## TL;DR

A new framework that eases usability by employing two-arm gestural electromyography instead of typical vision-based systems is proposed, enabling an agile framework that caters the needs of the signer-user.
