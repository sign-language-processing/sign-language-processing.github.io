# Signbuddy: from sign language research to scalable co-created solutions

**Year:** [[2026]]
**Topics:** [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]], [[topics/linguistics|Linguistics]], [[topics/education|Education]]

## TL;DR

SignBuddy, a co-created research tool developed through apartnership between the Flemish Sign Language Centre, a deaf-led organisation, and Ghent University, is presented, and a scalable one-shot sign language recognition method is proposed to achieve state-of-the-art results.
