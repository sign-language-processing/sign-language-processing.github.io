# An Isolated-Signing RGBD Dataset of 100 American Sign Language Signs Produced by Fluent ASL Signers

**Year:** [[2020]]
**Topics:** [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]], [[topics/linguistics|Linguistics]]
**Authors:** Saad Hassan; Larwan Berke; Elahe Vahdani; Longlong Jing; Yingli Tian; Matt Huenerfauth

## Links
- [Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org/paper/71a7c4329cf895d231e7b61106e904a134086170)
- [ACL Anthology](https://aclanthology.org/2020.signlang-1.14)

## TL;DR

A new dataset consisting of color and depth videos of fluent American Sign Language (ASL) signers performing sequences of 100 ASL signs from a Kinect v2 sensor is collected and released for use by the research community.
