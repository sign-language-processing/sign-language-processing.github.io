**Year:** [[2008]]
**Topics:** [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]], [[topics/linguistics|Linguistics]]
**Authors:** Jan Bungeroth; D. Stein; Philippe Dreuw; H. Ney; Sara Morrissey; Andy Way; L. V. Zijl

## TL;DR

The ATIS sign language corpus can be used for different tasks like automatic statistical translation and automatic sign language recognition and it allows the specific modeling of spatial references in signing space.
