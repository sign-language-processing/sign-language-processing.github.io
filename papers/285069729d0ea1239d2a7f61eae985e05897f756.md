# Modeling Image Variability in Appearance-Based Gesture Recognition

**Year:** [[2006]]
**Topics:** [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]]
**Authors:** Philippe Dreuw; Thomas Deselaers; Daniel Keysers; H. Ney

## Links
- [Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org/paper/285069729d0ea1239d2a7f61eae985e05897f756)

## TL;DR

The use of appearance-based features in hidden Markov model emission probabilities to recognize dynamic gestures in videos are introduced and promising first results on a novel database of the German finger-spelling alphabet are obtained.
