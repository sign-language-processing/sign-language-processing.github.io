# Defining meaningful units. Challenges in sign segmentation and segment-meaning mapping (short paper)

**Year:** [[2021]]
**Topics:** [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]], [[topics/linguistics|Linguistics]]
**Authors:** Mirella De Sisto; D. Shterionov; Irene Murtagh; Myriam Vermeerbergen; L. Leeson

## Links
- [Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org/paper/dba1736f1d729dbe9206527c0a278f3f9ddbc235)
- [ACL Anthology](https://aclanthology.org/2021.mtsummit-at4ssl.11)

## TL;DR

An overview of the linguistic properties of SL, such as coarticulation and simultaneity, which make these tasks complex are given and a proposal for mapping segments to meaning in the form of an agglomerate of lexical and non-lexical information is introduced.
