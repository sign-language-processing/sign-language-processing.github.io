# Dynamic affine-invariant shape-appearance handshape features and classification in sign language videos

**Year:** [[2013]]
**Topics:** [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]], [[topics/mathematics|Mathematics]], [[topics/linguistics|Linguistics]]

## TL;DR

The novel approach of dynamic affine-invariant shape-appearance model (Aff-SAM) is proposed and employed for handshape classification and sign recognition in sign language (SL) videos, offering a compact and descriptive representation of hand configurations as well as regularized model-fitting, assisting hand tracking and extracting handshape features.
