## TL;DR

The AuraLLM model is proposed, which leverages a decoupled architecture with CNText2Sign's pose data for novel direct gesture accuracy assessment and achieves all-scenario production with controllable coordination of gestures and facial expressions via pose-conditioned video synthesis.
