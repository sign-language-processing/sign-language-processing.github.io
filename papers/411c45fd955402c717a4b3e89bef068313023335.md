# Improving realism in automated fingerspelling of American sign language

**Year:** [[2021]]

## TL;DR

The development and evaluation of a new collision avoidance algorithm that aids fingerspelling that capitalizes on the transitions of individual fingers, which is efficient enough to support real-time fingerspelling while still maintaining a high level of predictive accuracy.
