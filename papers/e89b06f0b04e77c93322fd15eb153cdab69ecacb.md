# Dicta-Sign-LSF-v2: Remake of a Continuous French Sign Language Dialogue Corpus and a First Baseline for Automatic Sign Language Processing

**Year:** [[2020]]
**Topics:** [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]], [[topics/linguistics|Linguistics]]
**Authors:** V. Belissen; Annelies Braffort; M. Gouiffès

## Links
- [Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org/paper/e89b06f0b04e77c93322fd15eb153cdab69ecacb)
- [ACL Anthology](https://aclanthology.org/2020.lrec-1.740)

## TL;DR

This paper proposes a newer version of the publicly available SL corpus Dicta-Sign, limited to its French Sign Language part, and provides a baseline for the recognition of lexical signs and non-lexical structures on this corpus.
