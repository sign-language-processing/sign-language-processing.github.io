# Handling multimodality and scarce resources in sign language machine translation

**Year:** [[2016]]
**Topics:** [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]], [[topics/linguistics|Linguistics]]

## TL;DR

This thesis improves the automatic alignment between the annotated signs and their translations in the spoken language by applying a morphosyntactic and a semantic analysis and bridging the differences to find corresponding signs and phrases.
