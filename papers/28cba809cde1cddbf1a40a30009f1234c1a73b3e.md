# A Novel Approach to Managing Lower Face Complexity in Signing Avatars

**Year:** [[2022]]
**Topics:** [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]]
**Authors:** John C. McDonald; Ronan Johnson; Rosalee J. Wolfe

## Links
- [Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org/paper/28cba809cde1cddbf1a40a30009f1234c1a73b3e)
- [ACL Anthology](https://aclanthology.org/2022.sltat-1.10)

## TL;DR

The approach manages the complexity of creating lip postures, thus making fewer demands on the artists making them, and will be integral to the efforts to develop libraries containing lip posture to support the generation of facial expressions for several sign languages.
