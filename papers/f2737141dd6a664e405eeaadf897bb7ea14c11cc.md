# Benchmark Databases for Video-Based Automatic Sign Language Recognition

**Year:** [[2008]]
**Topics:** [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]], [[topics/linguistics|Linguistics]]
**Authors:** Philippe Dreuw; C. Neidle; V. Athitsos; S. Sclaroff; H. Ney

## Links
- [Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org/paper/f2737141dd6a664e405eeaadf897bb7ea14c11cc)
- [ACL Anthology](https://aclanthology.org/L08-1469)

## TL;DR

A new, linguistically annotated, video database for automatic sign language recognition is presented, which includes the new RWTH-BOSTON-400 corpus, which consists of 843 sentences, several speakers and separate subsets for training, development, and testing.
