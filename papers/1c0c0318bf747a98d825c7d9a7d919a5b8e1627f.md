**Topics:** [[topics/medicine|Medicine]], [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]], [[topics/linguistics|Linguistics]]

## TL;DR

SignStream is described, a computer program that is designed to facilitate transcription and linguistic analysis of visual language and machine vision methods to assist linguists in detailed annotation of gestures of the head, face, hands, and body are being developed.
