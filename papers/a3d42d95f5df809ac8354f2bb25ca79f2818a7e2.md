# KoSign Sign Language Translation Project: Introducing The NIASL2021 Dataset

**Year:** [[2022]]
**Topics:** [[topics/linguistics|Linguistics]], [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]]
**Authors:** Mathew Huerta-Enochian; Du Hui Lee; Hye Jin Myung; Kang-Suk Byun; Jun Woo Lee

## Links
- [Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org/paper/a3d42d95f5df809ac8354f2bb25ca79f2818a7e2)
- [ACL Anthology](https://aclanthology.org/2022.sltat-1.9)

## TL;DR

A new sign language production (SLP) and sign language translation (SLT) dataset, NIASL2021, consisting of 201,026 Korean-KSL data pairs is introduced and it is found that text-based prompting had a negative effect on translation quality in terms of naturalness and comprehension.
