## Abstract

This project's aim is to provide a low-cost option for speech-impaired people and develop a way for sign language users and non-sign language users to communicate. The primary goal is to build a system which transforms a sign language user's gesture into an automated text. This solution employs hand movement recognition which LSTMs works sophisticatedly to recognize and interpret dynamic hand movements with high precision. The system translates sign language into spoken words in real time, creating an effective means of communication and enhancing interaction and empowers voiceless individuals to communicate more effectively with others.
