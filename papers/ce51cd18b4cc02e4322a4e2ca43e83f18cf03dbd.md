# Towards Large Vocabulary Kazakh-Russian Sign Language Dataset: KRSL-OnlineSchool

**Year:** [[2022]]
**Topics:** [[topics/medicine|Medicine]], [[topics/linguistics|Linguistics]], [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]]
**Authors:** Medet Mukushev; A. Kydyrbekova; V. Kimmelman; A. Sandygulova

## Links

## TL;DR

This paper presents a new dataset for Kazakh-Russian Sign Language (KRSL) created for the purposes of Sign Language Processing that contains video recordings of Kazakhstan’s online school translated to Kazakh- Russian sign language by 7 interpreters.
