# Towards Bidirectional Conversion between Arabic Sign Language and Speech/Text

**Year:** [[2023]]

## TL;DR

An overview of the sign language and other ways used in communication between deaf and non deaf people as well as factors involved in this communication are provided and the approach, which ensures bidirectional conversion between Arabic sign language and speech/text is described.
