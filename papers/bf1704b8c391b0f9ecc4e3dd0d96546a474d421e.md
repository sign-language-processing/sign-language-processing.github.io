# Continuous sign language recognition enhanced by dynamic attention and maximum backtracking probability decoding

**Year:** [[2024]]
**Topics:** [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]]
**Authors:** Sije Xiong; Chunlong Zou; Juntong Yun; Du Jiang; Li Huang; Ying Liu; Yuan-liang Xie

## TL;DR

A comprehensive CSLR model (DAM-MCD) that combines a Dynamic Attention Mechanism and Maximum Backtracking Probability Dynamic Decoding, enhancing the model's inference capability and robustness is proposed and results show that the model effectively captures sign language motion information in videos.
