# Towards Inclusive Communication: A Unified LLM-Based Framework for Sign Language, Lip Movements, and Audio Understanding

**Year:** [[2025]]
**Topics:** [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]], [[topics/linguistics|Linguistics]]
**Authors:** J. Yeo; Hyeongseop Rha; Sungjune Park; Junil Won; Y. Ro

## TL;DR

This paper introduces the first unified framework capable of handling diverse combinations of sign language, lip movements, and audio for spoken-language text generation and reveals that explicitly modeling lip movements as a separate modality significantly improves SLT performance.
