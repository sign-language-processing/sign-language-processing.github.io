# Information structure in Russian Sign Language and Sign Language of the Netherlands

**Year:** [[2015]]
**Topics:** [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]], [[topics/linguistics|Linguistics]]
**Authors:** V. Kimmelman

## Links

## TL;DR

This dissertation explores Information Structure in two sign languages: Sign Language of the Netherlands and Russian Sign Language and shows that topics can be marked syntactically and prosodically in both languages, although some differences between the languages are found.
