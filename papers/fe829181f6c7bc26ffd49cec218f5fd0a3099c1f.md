# Generalizing a model for animating adverbs of manner in American Sign Language

**Year:** [[2021]]
**Topics:** [[topics/computer-science|Computer Science]], [[topics/linguistics|Linguistics]]
**Authors:** Robyn Moncrief

## TL;DR

It is shown that a model produced to generate adverbs of manner can be generalized and applied to a variety of neutral animated signs for avatar sign language synthesis and extensions for generalizability to the model are discussed.
